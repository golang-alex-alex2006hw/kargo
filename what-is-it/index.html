    # What is it ?

    kargo allows you to deliver your own data in a few minutes using Geospatial Open Standards such as WMS, WMTS, WCS, WFS... Indeed, kargo relies on well known Open Source geospatial solutions and takes advantages of their strongest features:

    1. PostGis
    2. GeoServer
    3. MapProxy
    4. TileServer-GL

    Kargo is designed to be deployed on Docker based infrastructures and specifically on Docker Swarm cluster. Benefit from Kaabah to create your infrastructure and manage your platform of services with ease.

    Sponsored by Kalisio

    Kargo was initiated by Kalisio to provide us with the necessary Geospatial Services we need to support our development.
